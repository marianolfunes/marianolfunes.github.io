Spend Compliance Agent

When a procurement contract is signed, its terms are loaded into the ERP by hand. Across several countries or regions, the purchase orders drift from the contract — in price, in scope, in whether they reference it at all. This agent reads the contract and checks the spend against it.

Example — a double charge
Contract
Day rate $1,500 — all-inclusive
Transport, mobilization and materials are inside the rate.
Purchase order
Line 1 — day rate$1,500 ✓
Line 2 — transport$400 ✗

Line 2 bills $400 for transport the day rate already covers. A line-by-line check passes it; read against the contract, it's a double charge.

Architecture

Contract Interpretationsubagent
Reads contract PDFs. Extracts pricing, UOM, scope — and the inclusions and exclusions that define what each rate covers.
pdf-reader · db-manager · schema-validator · confidence-scorer
2
PO Processinginline
Loads the ERP extract, normalizes it via field mapping, routes each PO by available matching path.
excel-loader · field-mapper · db-manager · schema-validator
3
Matchingsubagent
Links each PO to its contract — direct lookup, or semantic matching when there is no reference. "Crane services" matches "lifting and hoisting"; "crane inspection" does not.
db-manager · fuzzy-matcher · confidence-scorer
4
Compliancesubagent
Scores linkage, price and scope per PO. Holds the contract's inclusions and exclusions in context to catch overlaps and double charges.
db-manager · price-comparator · uom-converter · clustering-engine
5
Report Generationinline
Queries the database and formats the compliance report. Deliverables are queries, not separate analysis.
db-manager · report-writer
